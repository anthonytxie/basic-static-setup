Access Through Exchange APIs

You don't have to send us your money. Pair HODLbot up to the exchanges like Binance and Bitfinex, select an amount of coins you would like to HODLbot to manage, and HODLbot can start making trades. We take security extremely seriously will never reveal your secret API key.

Pick your Long Term Strategy

Pick a long term strategy that works for you. Select pre-made portfolios such as top 50 market cap, protocol coins, Asian coins, or fast transaction speed coins. If you can't find what you're looking for, make your own.

Dollar Cost Average

Don't time the market. Buy a fixed dollar amount for your portfolio on a regular time interval. It's proven to lower your average entry price.

Take Profits & Rebalance

Set profit taking targets so that if a coin blows up, you can re-invest or liquidate your profits. HODLbot can automatically trade back to BTC/ETC when profit targets are met.
